Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

This policy explains how Vega AI handles your information. I built Vega AI to help you track job applications and organize your job search, and I want to be clear about what data gets collected and how it's used.

What Vega AI does

There are two main tools:

Web application (vega.benidevo.com): Where you track job applications, manage your profile, and get recommendations. You can also run this on your own server if you prefer.

Browser extension: Captures job postings from LinkedIn with one click and sends them to your Vega AI account.

Information that gets collected

When you use the browser extension

The extension only collects job information when you click the capture button on a LinkedIn job page. It grabs:

  • Job title and company name
  • Job description and requirements
  • Location and job type (full-time, part-time, etc.)
  • The original job posting URL
  • Any notes you add before saving

The extension also stores your login information locally in your browser so you don't have to sign in repeatedly.

When you use the web application

Your job information gets stored (either through the extension or by adding jobs manually), along with:

  • Your account details (username, email if you use Google sign-in)
  • Your profile information (skills, experience, education)
  • Application tracking data (which jobs you've applied to, interview dates, etc.)
  • Notes and status updates you add to jobs

Technical stuff

The system uses Google's Gemini AI to analyze how well jobs match your profile and to help generate cover letter drafts. This means job descriptions and your profile information get sent to Google for processing, but only when you use these features.

How your information gets used

Job tracking: Your job data gets stored so you can organize and track your applications.

Smart recommendations: Job requirements get compared with your profile to suggest which opportunities might be the best fit.

Document help: Cover letter drafts can be generated based on the job requirements and your background.

Keeping you signed in: Your login gets remembered so the extension and web app work smoothly together.

What doesn't happen

Your information doesn't get sold to anyone. Your job data doesn't get shared with other companies (except Google's AI service when you use the recommendation features). Your browsing doesn't get tracked and you won't see ads.

Your choices

Self-hosting: You can run the entire system on your own server, which means your data never touches my servers. Instructions are on GitHub.

Data control: You can export your job data, delete specific jobs, or delete your entire account anytime.

Extension settings: You can configure the extension to work with either our cloud service or your own self-hosted setup.

Security

Data gets encrypted when transmitted between the extension and servers. Authentication tokens are stored securely in your browser. If you self-host, you control all the security measures.

Data location

Cloud version: Your data gets stored on Hetzner servers in Germany and may be processed in different locations for the AI features.

Self-hosted: Your data stays wherever you decide to run the system.

Open source

Both the browser extension and web application are open source under the AGPL-3.0 license. You can inspect the code, contribute improvements, or modify it for your own use. The source code is available on GitHub.

Third-party services

Google Fonts: The extension loads fonts from Google for consistent appearance.

Google OAuth: If you choose to sign in with Google (optional).

Google Gemini: When you use AI features like job matching or cover letter generation, job data gets sent to Google's AI service.

Kids

This service is intended for job seekers and isn't designed for anyone under 13.

Changes

This policy will be updated if needed and changes will be posted here. For major changes, I'll try to give you a heads up through the application.

Questions

If you have questions about privacy or how your data gets handled, email benjaminidewor@gmail.com or check out the code on GitHub.

Legal stuff

For EU users: Your data gets processed based on your consent (when you sign up and use the services) and legitimate interest (providing the job tracking functionality you requested). You can withdraw consent, request data deletion, or get copies of your data by contacting me.
